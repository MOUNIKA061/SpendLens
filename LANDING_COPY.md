# FAQ

## 1. Does SpendLens require login or credit card information?

No. SpendLens does not require authentication to generate an audit, and it never asks for payment information.

---

## 2. How does SpendLens calculate savings?

SpendLens compares your current subscriptions against vendor pricing, plan requirements, seat usage, and alternative tooling options using rule-based audit logic.

All pricing sources are pulled from official vendor pricing pages.

---

## 3. Is my company data public?

No.

Private information such as company name and email is excluded from public share links. Only sanitized audit summaries are shareable.

---

## 4. Does SpendLens replace financial software?

No.

SpendLens is focused specifically on AI tooling optimization, not general accounting or expense management.

---

## 5. Why are some audits marked as “already optimized”?

Because not every company is overspending.

SpendLens is designed to provide honest recommendations, even if the conclusion is that your current setup is already cost-efficient.

Artificially inflating savings would reduce trust in the audit.
