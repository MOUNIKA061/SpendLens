# SpendLens Metrics Strategy

SpendLens is not a daily-use SaaS product.

It is a high-intent B2B and B2C lead-generation tool designed to capture startups actively reviewing AI tooling costs. Because of that, the metrics strategy focuses on conversion quality and business outcomes instead of vanity engagement metrics like DAU or session time.

---

# North Star Metric

## Qualified Audit Completion Rate

### Definition

The percentage of completed audits that:

- Generate meaningful savings opportunities
- Capture contact information
- Result in a high-intent lead for Credex

Formula:

```text
Qualified Audit Completions / Total Audit Starts
```
